Lab


     
    If you're interested in working in the lab as a postdoc or full-time research staff, please send me a brief (~2 paragraph) message describing your research experience and interests. Include a CV, email addresses of up to three references, and any relevant manuscripts you've worked on (preprints ok!). I do not respond to messages that appear to have been written by ChatGPT or similar tools.
     
    UCI undergraduates: UCI students may join the lab at any time, when there are available research projects. Undergraduates are always welcome to attend lab meetings to learn more about our work - reach out for details. Advanced undergraduates may sign up for 199 course credit and/or an Honors project.
